Contact the team

Questions,Bugs or any other kind of comments are welcome in our SourceForge Forums, and/or our discussion mailing list.


You will need to register with SourceForge to post in the forums or create bug reports - but registeration is FREE.
You do not need to register if you just want to download or view the source code.

Plugin Development

If you've got questions related to plugin development then there are two places you can discuss your issue:

  • Plugin Developer's mailing list
  • Plugin Developer's Forum
The mailing list is better because the people on the list will receive your question, whilst the forum relies on people looking to see if there's anything new.

Donations

Donations are completely optional. Any donations will be used on software/hardware for development, testing and supporting of LCD Smartie. One aim is to get a real forum that is user friendly rather than using the sourceforge forums!
